About Our Hospital

Care Records is a modern healthcare institution delivering patient-first care backed by advanced diagnostics, an experienced medical team and a compassionate support staff. We combine clinical excellence with a comfortable, safe environment to make sure every visit focuses on recovery and wellbeing.

Who we are

Established with a mission to bring reliable healthcare to the community, Care Records provides outpatient, inpatient and emergency services along with specialized units for cardiology, orthopedics, neurology and more. We focus on evidence-based treatments and personalized patient plans.

Core strengths

  • 24/7 Emergency Department with trained staff
  • Advanced diagnostic imaging & pathology labs
  • Multi-specialty consultation and surgeries
  • Electronic patient records and secure data privacy
